Our project is about Wearables.
We designed a wearable jacket(Turn Signalling Jacket) for cyclists, it enables them to show directions to people behind them(Left/Right).

Components:
Arduino LillyPad
Lillypad Leds 
Pushbuttons
Conductive thread
